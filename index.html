About Us

Tell A Neva Holdings is a black owned and managed company that is diversified through a vast group of entities into which business is conducted, founded in the year 2018 Tell A Neva holdings has uplifted its conduct and since grown to be the company of choice towards its clientele.

The company was founded through the merger of Lomso Trading & Projects and S&K Group Trading and General which were both founded in the year 2012 and merged in the year 2018, both these companies were in the supply, trade and construction sectors of business and we at Tell A Neva Holdings have since carried forward our culture of being client centric, value friendly and just remarkable at what we do.
